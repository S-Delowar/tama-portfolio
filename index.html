Biography

Rahima Tanbin Tama is a current M.Sc. student in the Department of Biotechnology & Genetic Engineering at Islamic University, Kushtia-7003, Bangladesh, having previously earned a B.Sc. degree from the same department. She has been playing a role as a research assistant in the Laboratory of Molecular & Cellular Research, Islamic University, Kushtia-7003, Bangladesh, since 2023. Her good academic performance has earned her recognition and respect among her peers and professors. Her diverse research interests include Genetics, Microbiology, Molecular Biology, and Bioinformatics. Her dedication and passion for the discipline have led her to enroll in the Master’s program in the same department, with the aim of expanding her knowledge and expertise further.
